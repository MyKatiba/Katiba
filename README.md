Katiba is a Kotlin Multiplatform mobile application designed to empower Kenyan citizens through accessible civic education. Combining the engaging learning model of Duolingo with the content-rich experience of YouVersion, Katiba makes understanding the Kenyan Constitution intuitive, interactive, and rewarding.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between legal complexity and public understanding, enabling every Kenyan to become a more informed and engaged citizen.

## ğŸ¯ Key Features

### Home Tab - Daily Learning Hub
- **Clause of the Day**: Start your civic education journey with a featured constitutional clause updated daily
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Intelligent summaries explaining what each clause means and its significance
- **Civic Educator Videos**: Watch short, engaging video explanations from professional civic educators (uploaded daily via admin portal)
- **Practical Application**: AI-generated tips on how to leverage constitutional clauses in your daily life as a Kenyan citizen

Each card offers an immersive deep-dive experience when clicked, with intuitive navigation between text and video content.

### Constitution Tab - Digital Constitution Reader
- Browse the complete Kenyan Constitution like a digital book
- Navigate through chapters and clauses seamlessly
- Scroll through formatted constitutional text
- Similar experience to popular Bible reader apps

### Learning Plans Tab - Structured Civic Education
- Bite-sized lessons following the Constitution from beginning to end
- Gamified learning experience inspired by Duolingo
- Track progress through milestone-based modules
- Build consistency with streaks and achievements

### Profile Tab - Your Civic Journey
- View personal statistics (residence info, learning streak, achievements)
- Display earned badges and milestones
- Track your activity and learning progress
- Access settings via the cog icon in the top-right corner
