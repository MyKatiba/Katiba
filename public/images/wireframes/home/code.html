"Every person is equal before the law and has the right to equal protection and equal benefit of the law."

Constitution of Kenya, 2010

lightbulb Understanding this clause

This is the bedrock of equality in Kenya. It means no one is above the lawâ€”not the president, not the police, not the wealthy. It guarantees that the legal system must treat everyone the same way, offering the same defenses and the same punishments regardless of status.

It also introduces the concept of "equal benefit," meaning government services and opportunities should not favor specific groups over others.
